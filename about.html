About

I am Indra P Tiwari, PhD, a Professor of Development and Planning. I obtained MA Degree in Geography from Tribhuvan University, Nepal; MSc in Human Settlements Development and PhD in Rural and Regional Development Planning from the Asian Institute of Technology, Thailand.

I have experience working with eight universities in Nepal, Thailand and Japan including the National Institute of Development Administration, Thailand and the Tokyo Institute of Technology in Japan as a Fellow of the Japan Society for the Promotion of Science.

I have worked for the United Nations Economic and Social Commission for Asia and the Pacific (UN-ESCAP) as policy expert seconded by the Government of the Federal Republic of Germany. I also worked for GTZ, United Nations TCDC, NGO, and the Government of Nepal.

My diverse research interests include employment creation, income generation, poverty alleviation, prosperity creation and sustainable livelihood development; human settlement systems, spatial organization and urban development; transport and tourism; social and development policy among others.

I am also affiliated to a number of national and international professional and social organizations and institutions.
